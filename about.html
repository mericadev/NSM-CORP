LET'S WORK TOGETHER

I'm Nqobile, Your Favourite Coach

I HAVE CHANNELED MY PASSION FOR PEOPLE INTO COACHING. I PROVIDE CAREER AND LIFE COACH WITH A SPECIALISATION IN CAREER TRANSITIONING AND PERSONAL GROWTH. MY MISSION IS TO EMPOWER YOU TO DISCOVER YOUR GREATNESS AND TRANSFORM YOUR CAREER AND LIFE.

With over 10 years of experience in Human Capital I have helped individuals in different stages of their careers. Whether you are a recent graduate, aspiring entrepreur or going through a career or life transition, I can help you gain clarity, achieve career goal, overcome obstacles, build your self-confidence and introduce tools that you can use independently.

IT WASN'T ALWAYS LIKE THIS

IN THE JOURNEY OF BECOMING A CAREER COACH I HAVE EXPERIENCED CHALLENGES AND STRUGGLES AS WE ALL DO. LOOKING AT WHERE I AM NOW, I CAN HAPPILY ADMIT IT WAS ALL WORTH IT .

I started my career as an HR intern for a HR consulting company, where I learnt the importance of fostering authentic relationships with clients and further catering to the needs of the client in order to make a lasting impression. It was also where I further appreciate human behaviour and how the essence of human behaviour is what fascinates me.

Over the years, I realized my true passion lay in not just developing people in the workplace but also developing people holistically through life coaching, which led me to pursue my Masters qualification in Leadership coaching which has equipped me with the tools and models that enable me to tailor the coaching experience to each client.

My experiences working for large corporates in the financial and the management consulting industry, climbing the proverbial corporate ladder through making mistakes and learning from those experiences. Have provided me with a deep understanding of the obstacles professionals face and how to overcome them.
